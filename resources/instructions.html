How to Play Wordle!

Guessing the five lettered word in six tries may be hard for some, but not you, player!

After each guess the colour of the letter tiles will change. When a tile is green, the letter is in the correct position. When a tile is yellow, the letter is present within the word, but not in that position. And finally, when a tile is grey, the letter is not present within the secret word. Pressing the enter word will allow you to guess the next word.

Good luck!
